About this website

This website was created as a part of my client-side dynamic web class.
The goal is quite simple: it helps you follow your favorite tv shows as they air.
Towards this purpose, we fetch data about all the TV shows (from thetvdb), alert you about those you're subscribed to and help you find subtitles/download links for them. You may also rate & review the episodes you've watched.

Technologies used:

  • Bootstrap (with the Flatly theme)
  • jQuery
  • jQuery UI
  • jQuery Mobile
